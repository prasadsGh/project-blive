Eligibility and Verification

Eligibility Criteria-

You CAN donate plasma if-

  1. Your Age is between 18-65 years.
  2. Your Weight is 50kgs or above
  3. You have a prior diagnosis of COVID-19 (as documented by a diagnostic test)
  4. You have recovered from Covid-19 disease with complete resolution of symptoms at least 28 days before donation

You CANNOT donate plasma if-

  1. You have ever been pregnant (for women)
  2. You have uncontrolled diabetes or hypertension with change in medication in last 28 days
  3. You are a cancer survivor
  4. You have Chronic kidney/heart/lung/liver disease
